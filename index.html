Hoa M. Le

I am a research assistant/student at the Data Science Lab, Hanoi University of Science & Technology, reading and working on statistical machine learning. My research interests include deep neural networks and latent variable models, with focus on conditional generative image models, and multi-modal modelling with inference methods that endorse interpretability and scalability for downstream applications in other domains (e.g. computer vision, bioinformatics, recommendation).

In my academic journey, I have completed an M.Phil. in Computational Biology at Cambridge University (2014) and a B.Sc. in Mathematics with Finance at Exeter University (2013). Before that, I was a B.A. student at the Business school, National Economics University for one year.
