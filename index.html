Hoa M. Le

I am a research assistant/student at the Data Science Lab, Hanoi University of Science & Technology (HUST), reading and working on statistical machine learning. My research interests include deep neural networks and latent variable models, with focus on multi-modal modelling and inference methods that endorse interpretability and scalability for downstream applications in other domains (e.g. computer vision, bioinformatics, recommendation). I am also particularly interested in the advances of conditional generative image modelling - a branch of generative models which has contributed ample insights to structure learning, and inspiring applications in vision/graphics.

Prior to HUST, I had a brief but wonderful time at FPT Software Hanoi. In my academic journey, I have completed an M.Phil. in Computational Biology at Cambridge University (2014) and a B.Sc. in Mathematics with Finance at Exeter University (2013). Before that, I was a B.A. student at the Business school, National Economics University for one year.
