Computational Artist

2021
A proof of concept for incorporating AI-powered tools into a creative process, aiming to accelerate applicable workflows and - more importantly - make the experience more rewarding and fulfilling, especially for the typical person. Art practice and AI-assisted tools have been developed and collected by the author over the years.
